Travis's Favorite Movies List

My top 3 movies of all time:

Interstellar

If you're a huge fan of science this is the movie for you. A thriller combined with an emotional drama, this movie hits it completely out of the park.

Jurassic Park - The Lost World

Jurassic Park - The Lost World is a classic that the entire famaily can watch. At times Jeff golbloom mixes funny and seriousness, it's a blast that can be pretty intense.

Iron Man

If you're into super hero action, this movie is amoung the best. billionaire-philanthropist Tony Stark sets off on a journey to stop the immoral sale of his weapons to terrorists. A truly execellent film.
